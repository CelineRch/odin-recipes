Description

When making these soft and utterly delicious Finnish cinnamon buns (korvapuusti), sprinkled with pearl sugar, Finnish style, the amazing scent of cinnamon and cardamom takes over the kitchen. Follow the easy step-by-step recipe to create beautifully shaped cinnamon buns to enjoy with your coffee!


Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Fresh yeast
  • Caster sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Vanilla sugar
  • Egg
  • Bread flour
  • Sea salt
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Pearl sugar

Steps

  1. Making the dough
  2. Proofing the dough
  3. Rolling and filling the cinnamon buns
  4. Shaping the traditional Finnish Cinnamon buns (korvapuusti)
  5. Second proof
